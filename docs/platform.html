What does Leapfin Revenue Platform do?

Leapfin manages all of your revenue data from start to finish. We integrate with your finance systems so you can have clean revenue data for financial reporting.

Companies with high transaction volume and complexity trust Leapfin because:

  • Implementation is faster
  • There is no need for consultants or costly professional services
  • You won't have to force-fit a revenue framework into your database
  • You will reduce risk

Whether you're preparing for an IPO or audit, expanding to new geographies, or launching new revenue streams, Leapfin can empower your finance, accounting, and IT/Systems teams.

How do we do it?

Your worst nightmare is being tasked to build something that seems impossible.

You know it involves educating yourself in finance and accounting while adding tons of engineering overhead and costly space to your database.

Imagine getting accurate financials for your stakeholders. You've dreamt of how to do it before, but it either involves a ton of manual processes, building an internal database that takes 4 years, or customizing your ERP system. You just want to automate it in a system that is an expert in your revenue model and your accounting logic. You're not sure how, you just know there has to be a better way.

There is. Our Leapfin Revenue Platform is comprised of our Financial Data Platform and Revenue Subledger. Together, they work together in a 4-step process to create those accurate reports you've needed for years.

Leapfin Revenue Platform

  • Our Financial Data Platform consolidates data from your existing financial systems and standardizes your data models.

  • Our Revenue Subledger automates transaction-level journal entries based on business events and spits out summary journal entries into your general ledger on a daily basis.

Revenue data integration

Data management & Enrichement

Revenue Subledger

Revenue accounting rule engine

Apply accounting policies in pre-built rules templates to generate transaction-level journal entries

Your business is not static - it changes everyday. So, why would your revenue accounting rules stay the same?

Through years of experience, we know the right balance between pre-built templates and flexibility. Rules can be stacked so you don't have to worry about code changes. Trust the rules that were battle-tested by auditors and high growth companies just like yours.

Revenue Subledger

Reporting & data sharing

Your summarized data is transfered to your external systems to report accurate revenue data and create custom reports

Your cleaned revenue data is accessible to your ERP, BI tool, and data warehouse. With our flexible graph data model, we can add metadata as needed to slice and dice in reports.

Why our technology is better

You won't find a financial data platform or revenue subledger anywhere else. We dare you to google it. So, what makes us so special?

  • Excel and ERP extensions can't scale with your transaction volume

    Leapfin is built to handle massive data volume in the digital era. With multiple data repositories for various data accessing and processing use cases, we can handle millions of transactions without slowing down

  • With our Financial Record Graph you can link together data from multiple systems

    You can create and track the entire journey of a financial transaction Link business events such as discounts to invoices and refunds to payments

  • Other platforms don't have flexible data models - but we do

    We incorporate learnings from our customers to accommodate diverse use cases and future-proof them for business growth

  • You can finally access all the transaction-level details in our revenue subledger

    Automated transaction-level journal entries, reconciliation, and matching allows you to get pinpoint accuracy and reconcile down to $0

  • No need for professional services and there's 0 hidden costs

    Leapfin has an extensive library of pre-packaged rules and processing logic that translates into journal entries. Leapfin processes journal entries in the correct order. You won't have to rely on professional services and extra customization

  • Don't worry about data getting overwritten

    With data changing over time, and without an audit trail, is a nightmare for accountants. Leapfin ensures changes are append-only so that every change to a transaction is traceable
