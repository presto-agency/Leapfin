Security & Governance

Overview

Leapfin understands that data security is of utmost priority to our customers. To ensure that our customers have confidence in the security of Leapfin, we leverage industry standard security solutions and best practices, keep our customers well informed, and quickly respond to security issues when they arise.

Leapfin makes regular and efficient use of modern risk assessment techniques and security management tools, and are constantly evaluating the threat landscape and adjusting our information security on a timely basis.

Leapfin’s IT infrastructure is protected by layers of controls, from industry-standard firewalls, network, and host-based intrusion detection systems, to behavioral analytics tools and other technologies sourced from leading information security vendors
